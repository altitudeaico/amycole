## How she sells it (steal these)

### 1. Objection-handling baked into the sales page
The Seedance page (screenshot 06) answers the exact hesitations in the buyer's head, on the page:
- "DO I NEED TO KNOW ANYTHING ABOUT AI OR VIDEO EDITING? No. If you can copy and paste, you can use this."
- "DO I NEED SEEDANCE 2.0 SEPARATELY?" (honestly explains the tool dependency)
- "WILL THIS WORK IN FRENCH?"

Amy's Phantom sales page does NOT yet do this. **Action: add an FAQ/objection block to the Phantom sales page.**

### 2. The upsell logic on Privé Maison Elevée (the most important screenshot, 05)
Her copy tells her own buyers that the course they bought is only step one:
- "YOU BUILT THE AI INFLUENCER, now BUILD THE EMPIRE."
- "Most AI creators are sitting on a goldmine they don't even know EXISTS... you're leaving 80% of the money on the table."
- "Selling AI courses to other AI creators IS a business model. It's just not a LONG-TERM business model. It's a starting point."
- The pitch: turn the AI influencer into a multi-stream branded business (home / fitness / food / fashion niche → brand deals + affiliate + products).

This is her monetising the exact strategic insight from our own research: **the consistency method is commoditised; the character + niche is the real asset.** She built a $57 product around that argument alone.

### 3. Voice matches persona
Aspirational, feminine, emoji-heavy, hype-but-friendly. Deliberate and consistent. It IS the character.

## Implications for Amy (the counter-positioning)

1. **Amy's £24 single PDF is the entry rung of a ladder sacredlysavage has already built 10 rungs of.** She validates the price points above: $47 story bot, $57 monetisation upsell, $67 prompt studio, $97 done-for-you avatar, $147 flagship. These are proven-converting rungs Amy can build toward.

2. **She hands us the counter-position for free.** She IS the crowded lane: blonde, thirst-adjacent, hype-voiced, "another AI creator selling AI courses" (which she herself admits is not long-term). Amy's design — operator-brain, dry, posh, quiet-luxury, "looks like an influencer but thinks like a founder" — is the deliberate antidote. Where she's loud, Amy's restrained. Where she's thirst-luxury, Amy's boardroom-luxury. **That's a wedge, not just a style.**

3. **The durable money is NOT the consistency method.** Her PME page proves people pay for "now turn your character into a multi-stream brand." That's Amy's path B (the higher-value product). Validated at $57.
